Monster Survivors

Face endless waves of creatures in this thrilling online action survival game.

About The Game

Dive into the intense action of Monster Survivors, where you are the last stand against relentless hordes of menacing creatures. Your mission is simple: survive as long as you can. For every monster you defeat, you'll gain precious experience, level up, and unlock powerful new abilities and devastating upgrades. Choose your path wisely to build an unstoppable force against the ever-increasing threat.

How to Play

The controls are designed to be simple and intuitive, letting you focus on the action.

  • Movement: Use your mouse to guide your character or press the W A S D keys.
  • Attacking: Your character attacks automatically. Focus on positioning and dodging!
  • Level Up: Collect the glowing gems dropped by defeated enemies to gain experience.
  • Upgrades: When you level up, you'll be presented with a choice of new abilities. Choose carefully to create powerful synergies.

Key Features

  • Endless Action: Fight against ever-growing hordes of monsters.
  • Deep Upgrade System: Hundreds of potential ability combinations.
  • Instant Play: Free to play directly in your browser with no download required.
  • Fully Responsive: Enjoy a seamless experience on both PC and mobile devices.
